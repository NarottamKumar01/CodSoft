Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema. He was born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, to Krishna Kumar Singh and Usha Singh, and had four sisters named Neetu, Meetu, Priyanka, and Shweta.Rajput began his career in theatre and television, making his debut in the romantic drama "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil" in 2008. He gained widespread recognition for his role as Manav Deshmukh in the soap opera "Pavitra Rishta" from 2009 to 2011.He transitioned to films with "Kai Po Che!" in 2013, for which he received three Best Male Debut awards. Other notable films include "PK" (2014), "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" (2016), "Kedarnath" (2018), "Sonchiriya" (2019), and "Chhichhore" (2019). His last film, "Dil Bechara," was released posthumously in 2020.Rajput was also known for his versatility and was occasionally referred to as the "next SRK" (Shah Rukh Khan). He was featured twice on Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list and was regarded as one of the most talented actors of his generation.He died by suicide on June 14, 2020, at the age of 34, in his home in Bandra, Mumbai. The Central Bureau of Investigation ruled out any foul play in the case.Before his acting career, Rajput had secured admission in Delhi Technological University to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering but dropped out to pursue his passion for acting.His personal life included a six-year live-in relationship with Ankita Lokhande, his co-star from "Pavitra Rishta," which ended in 2015.
